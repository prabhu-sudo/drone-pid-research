# Week 2 — Electronics Assembly and Soldering

## Summary

Attached the vibration-dampening grommets to the FC/ESC stack (supplied with the BotWing F722 + BotDrive AM32 combo). Soldered all four motors to their corresponding ESC pads, and soldered the main battery lead and capacitor to the ESC positive and negative power pads.

## Work Done

- Mounted grommets between FC and ESC layers of the stack for vibration isolation.
- Soldered motor phase wires to the correct ESC motor outputs (4 motors, 3 wires each).
- Soldered the main power leads (battery wire) to the ESC positive (+) and negative (-) copper pads.
- Soldered the capacitor across the same positive/negative pads to suppress voltage spikes and electrical noise on the power rail.

## Notes

This was the most failure-sensitive part of the build — wrong polarity on the capacitor or battery leads at this stage can damage the ESC or FC immediately on power-up. Joints were checked visually and for solder bridging before first power-on (covered in Week 3).
